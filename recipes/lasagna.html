Lasagna

Discription

A classic lasagna recipe, a simple dish and easy to prepare.

Preperation Time

approx 30 minutes

Cooking Time

over 2 hours

Servings

servers 8-10

Ingredients

For the ragu/sauce

  1. 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. 900g/2lb lean beef mince
  3. 2 onions, roughly chopped
  4. 4 sticks of celery, diced (optional)
  5. 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  6. 2 level tbsp plain flour
  7. 150ml or 1/4 pint beef stock
  8. 1 tbsp redcurrent jelly (optional) or 1 tsp caster sugar
  9. 3 tbsp tomato puree
  10. 1 tbsp chopped thyme
  11. 2 X 400g tins of chopped tomatos

White Sauce

  1. 50g/2oz butter
  2. 50g/20s plain flour
  3. 750ml/1 1/4 pint hot milk
  4. 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  5. 50g/2oz parmesan, finely grated
  6. salt and ground black pepper to taste
